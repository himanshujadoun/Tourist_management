Sri Nagar

Srinagar, the summer capital of the Indian state of Jammu and Kashmir, is a picturesque paradise nestled in the Himalayan region. It is renowned for its stunning Dal Lake, where houseboats gently float and vibrant shikara boats navigate through lotus-covered waters. The city is a mosaic of Shalimar Bagh and Nishat Bagh, offering a glimpse into its rich history. Srinagar's old town boasts charming narrow streets and historic architecture, while the Shankaracharya Temple provides panoramic views of the city and the surrounding mountains. The region's natural beauty, with snow-capped peaks and lush valleys, makes Srinagar a favorite destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.
