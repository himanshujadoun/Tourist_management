Place Information

Amritsar

The Golden Temple, also known as Sri Harmandir Sahib, is a spiritual and architectural marvel in Amritsar, India. Adorned with gold leaf and situated in the middle of a sacred pool, it's the holiest shrine for Sikhs. Visitors are drawn to its serene ambiance, captivating reflections on the water, and melodious Kirtan filling the air. The temple's welcoming Langar serves thousands of meals daily to people of all backgrounds.
