Agra

Agra, located in Uttar Pradesh, India, is renowned for its historical treasures. The Taj Mahal, an iconic white marble mausoleum built by Emperor Shah Jahan, stands as a testament to love and architectural mastery. The Red Fort, or Lal Qila, is another prominent attraction, constructed by Emperor Akbar in the 16th century and known for its imposing red sandstone architecture. Fatehpur Sikri, a nearby city founded by Akbar, boasts a blend of Mughal architectural styles and includes the Jama Masjid and the grand Buland Darwaza. These UNESCO World Heritage Sites collectively showcase India's rich Mughal history and artistic heritage.
